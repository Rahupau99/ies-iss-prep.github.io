How to use this library
1
Keep all HTML files in the same folder on your device — this index links to them by filename.
2
Open any paper and go to the Practice tab. Write your answer, then reveal the model answer and rate yourself.
3
Use the Mock tab for a timed 3-hour full-paper simulation with a question jump grid.
4
Progress for each paper is saved separately in your browser — it won't mix across subjects.
5
Open index.html in Safari (iPad) or Chrome (Android) and tap Add to Home Screen for instant access offline.
